About

Well, Hello There! My name is Miles Echavez. I am a former healthcare worker turned software developer. Coding has developed into a passion of mine especially in the area of front-end development. I have dedicated my time to continually gain proficiency in all aspects of web development in order to improve myself as a developer and problem solver.

Feel free to browse my projects, skills declarations, and services sections and then send over your information so we can talk!

Greetings from Vermont! I graduated from Quinnipiac University in 2018 with a Bachelors in Health Science. I completed my degree and very shortly thereafter began a stint as an nurse aid in Connecticut. I followed this up with advanced practice training as a certified nursing assistant which I have been for the past year. However, I've always felt a greater calling, in particular to the field of Front-End Development.

After building my own PC and following closely the rapid and exciting expansion of the tech industry, I started taking classes on Code Academy, and the rest is history! My training in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, alongside other frameworks and asssociated programming training has allowed me to enjoy significant progress which can be shown below. I'd be thrilled to have a part in developing and maintaining one of the many sites users view around the world.
